About Us

Welcome to Al Shifaa Medical Center, a trusted healthcare provider committed to delivering quality medical services since our establishment in 2005. At Al Shifaa, we prioritize the well-being of our patients, offering a comprehensive range of medical services with a focus on personalized care. Our dedicated team of experienced healthcare professionals is here to provide compassionate and patient-centered treatment, ensuring that each individual receives the attention and support they deserve. With a commitment to excellence, Al Shifaa Medical Center continues to be a beacon of health and healing in our community, striving to make a positive impact on the lives we touch. Thank you for choosing Al Shifaa Medical Center for your healthcare needs. Your health, our priority.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the premier healthcare provider facility in Jordan in delivering quality and compassionate care to all patients while maintaining highest medical ethical standards at all times

Our Mission

To provide medical and diagnostic services to citizens of Jordan through adopting excellent health care delivery standards, utilizing the latest technology, hiring qualified staff and continuously improving their competencies. We are highly dedicated to exceed the expectation of all our stakeholders and strive to maintain a safe environment for our customers.
